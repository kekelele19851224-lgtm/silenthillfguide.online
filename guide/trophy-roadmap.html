Silent Hill f – Trophy Guide & Roadmap

This guide helps you achieve Platinum by unlocking all 57 trophies in Silent Hill f. It includes difficulty stacking rules, a 3-playthrough strategy, Lost in the Fog tips, and how all collectible systems tie into trophies. Based on PowerPyx and verified trophy sources.

Trophy Overview

  • Total: 57 trophies — 1 Platinum, 1 Gold, 9 Silver, 46 Bronze.
  • Online Trophies: 0 (all are offline)
  • Estimated difficulty: 5–6 / 10; Platinum time: ~30-40 hours
  • Missable trophies: 36 (non-story trophies are often missable)
  • Puzzle difficulty trophies do not stack — you must complete each puzzle difficulty separately.
  • Action difficulty trophies do stack — doing Lost in the Fog action covers lower difficulties.

3-Playthrough Strategy to Platinum

  1. Run 1 (Story / Story): First playthrough doing the default ending (Ending 1) and gathering as many collectibles and misc trophies as possible.
  2. Run 2 (NG+): Aim for Ending 3 (no Red Capsules, take Sacred Sword, but don't purify) while pursuing more trophies, including puzzle difficulties.
  3. Run 3 (NG+): With at least two endings recorded, go for Ending 4 (no Red Capsules, get & purify Sacred Sword) and mop up remaining trophies including Lost in the Fog content. Also trigger the UFO ending on side branch.

UFO Ending Steps (for Trophy)

  1. Listen to the UFO Radio Broadcast in Ebisugaoka early.
  2. Read all three 'The Great Space Invasion' movie posters in order and then read the matching movie review.
  3. Later, clear the monster alley near the general store and interact with the UFO object to trigger the ending.

Lost in the Fog Difficulty & Tips

  • Use perfect dodges and counters rather than spamming attacks.
  • Manage your Omamori and inventory — bring healing items and panic recovery tools.
  • Don't rush — explore thoroughly for upgrades and collectibles to make later fights easier.

Key Trophy Categories

Story Progression Trophies

These are unmissable and earned through natural story progression. Focus on exploring thoroughly during these sections to collect items and documents.

Ending Trophies

You need all 5 endings for the Platinum. Plan your playthroughs around the ending requirements, especially the Red Capsules restriction and Sacred Sword states.

Difficulty Trophies

Remember that action difficulty stacks (Lost in the Fog covers all), but puzzle difficulty does not stack (must do each separately).

Collectible Trophies

Many trophies require finding specific items, documents, or upgrades. Use a collectibles guide to ensure you don't miss any during your playthroughs.

Collectibles & Trophy Interaction

Many trophies are tied to collectibles: character upgrades (Ema), Inari-doors & boxes, upgrade items, documents. Be sure to follow a 100% route guide while pursuing trophies to avoid missing things.

Checklist Summary

  1. Complete all puzzle difficulties (Story / Hard / Lost in the Fog).
  2. Complete all action difficulties (stacking helps).
  3. Unlock all 5 endings (including the UFO ending).
  4. Collect all trophy-relevant collectibles across runs.

Important Notes

Save Management: Create multiple save files before major decisions like consuming Red Capsules or choosing ending paths. This allows you to backtrack if needed for specific trophies.
NG+ Benefits: New Game Plus retains character upgrades and unlocks new content paths. Use this to your advantage when planning trophy runs.
